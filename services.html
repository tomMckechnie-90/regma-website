Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build the website?

The timeline depends on the package and your requirements. Most Signature Packages are completed in 2–4 weeks, while E-commerce sites may take 4–6 weeks.

Are there any hidden costs?

No. All pricing is transparent and includes everything listed in your chosen package. After the first year, you can opt for an affordable care plan for ongoing hosting and support.

What do I need to provide to get started?

I’ll need some information about your business, your goals, and any branding assets (like logos or photos). If you’re unsure, I can guide you through the process.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Every site I build is fully responsive and looks great on all devices.

What’s included in the maintenance package?

The Regma Care Package covers security updates, backups, uptime monitoring, and performance checks to keep your site running smoothly.

Can you help with SEO?

Basic SEO setup is included in all packages to help your site get found on Google and other search engines.

What if I need extra features or custom functionality?

I’m happy to discuss custom requirements and can provide a tailored quote for additional features.
