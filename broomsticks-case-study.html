Broomsticks Cleaning Services

Project Details

  • Client: Broomsticks
  • Services: Signature Package
  • Year: 2025
  • Website: Live Site

The Challenge

As a new local business, Broomsticks needed a professional online presence to go with there presence on social media. The challenge was to design a clean, multi-page website that reflected the brand's high-quality, eco-conscious identity while strategically encouraging visitors to get in touch directly via phone, socials and email for all bookings.

The Solution

I developed a clean, fast, and fully responsive 5-page website that feels professional and trustworthy. Key contact information is featured prominently in the navigation bar on every page, making it incredibly easy for customers to take the next step.

The Results

The final website provides Broomsticks with a clean and professional website. It successfully directs all visitor traffic towards making a direct enquiry, helping the founder to secure initial clients and begin growing her new business.
