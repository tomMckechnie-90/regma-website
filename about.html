How it all began

In early 2024, after losing my mother Marge to cancer, I left my job in warehousing feeling lost. Her passing was the catalyst for a major change in my life. I knew I wanted to do something she would be proud of, and something that would give me more valuable time with my young family.

I decided to pursue a long-held interest in web design and enrolled in a 16-week software development bootcamp with Code Institute. It was there that the journey truly began. I fell in love with development, building projects, and taking part in hackathons with fellow coders in South Yorkshire.

After the bootcamp, I knew I wanted to take my new passion and turn it into a business that could help other people. That's when Regma Web Design was born.

The story behind the name

The name 'Regma' is more than just a name to me. It's an anagram of Marge, my mum's name. In late 2023, I lost my mum to cancer after a short battle. Her passing made me re-evaluate everything, and I knew I wanted to do something she would have been proud of. I wanted her memory to be woven into the business, so I played around with anagrams of her name and landed on 'Regma'.
